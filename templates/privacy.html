Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ last_updated or '2025-10-19' }}

Overview

We respect your privacy. Safe-URL-Check is designed to analyze URLs without collecting personal information beyond what is necessary to perform the scan and operate the service.

Data We Process

  • Submitted URL(s) for analysis and threat intelligence checks.
  • Technical metadata (e.g., HTTP headers, response codes) from target sites.
  • Operational telemetry such as request count and latency for system health.

We do not require account registration and do not intentionally collect names, emails, or other direct identifiers.

How We Use Data

  • Provide URL risk assessments and explainable signals.
  • Query external threat intelligence providers where configured.
  • Maintain reliability and detect abuse via rate limiting.

Retention

By default, scans are handled in-memory and not stored persistently. If optional logging is enabled in the future, we will only retain minimal data required for troubleshooting or improving detection quality, and for the shortest time necessary.

Security

We apply rate limits, validate inputs, and use secure requests to external providers. See our Security page for additional details.

Third-Party Services

When configured, we may query services such as Google Safe Browsing, URLHaus, and AlienVault OTX to enhance threat detection. Requests to these services include the URL being checked and may be subject to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

If you believe data has been collected about you inadvertently, or you would like to request deletion of logs related to your use, contact us using the details below.
