Our Commitment

Safe URL Check is built on a privacy-first architecture. We believe security tools should not compromise your privacy in the process of protecting you. This policy explains exactly what data we handle and how we protect it.

What We Process

  • Submitted URLs — The URL you enter is held in memory only for the duration of the scan (typically 1-3 seconds). It is never written to disk, logged, or retained in any form.
  • Technical Metadata — During analysis, we retrieve HTTP headers, SSL certificate data, and DNS records from the target site. This data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.
  • Aggregated Statistics — We maintain anonymized counts of total scans performed for capacity planning. This contains no URL data, no IP addresses, and no identifying information.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not store scan history
  • We do not log IP addresses
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not share data with third parties for marketing
  • We do not require user accounts or registration

Third-Party Services

To perform threat intelligence checks, we transmit submitted URLs to the following services:

  • Google Safe Browsing — URL reputation checking
  • URLhaus — Malware distribution database
  • AlienVault OTX — Threat intelligence platform

These services receive only the URL being scanned. Please review their respective privacy policies for details on their data handling practices.

Data Retention

Scan data exists only in server memory for the duration of the request (typically under 3 seconds). No persistent storage of any scan data occurs.
